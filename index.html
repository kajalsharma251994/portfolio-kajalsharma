Machine Learning - Predicting-Airline-Customer-Satisfaction

Objective

The main objective of this project is to analyze and predict factors influencing customer satisfaction in the airline industry using machine learning techniques. By identifying the most impactful service and operational variables, the project aims to provide actionable insights that airlines can use to enhance customer experience, strengthen loyalty, and maintain competitiveness in a challenging market.

Specific Goals

  • Identify Key Drivers of Satisfaction: Finding the most important factors—such as cleanliness, seat comfort, delays, and in-flight entertainment—that strongly influence whether passengers feel satisfied or dissatisfied.
  • Develop Predictive Models: To build machine learning models that can classify passengers as satisfied or dissatisfied, helping airlines anticipate customer experiences more accurately.
  • Provide Actionable Insights: To turn the analysis into practical recommendations so airlines can target improvements, enhance customer experience, and strengthen competitiveness.
